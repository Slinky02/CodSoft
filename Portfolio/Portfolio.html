About

Hi there, I'm Celine Gardner from South Africa. I am a graphic designer and training to be a Web Developer. I have done graphic design at school as an art subject and expanded my knowledge and skill freelancing in graphic design. I have started a Web Development course this year (2024) with FreeCodeCamp that I am still in the process of getting my qualified certificate.

My very first paying job was in high school as a waitress for weddings. I have managed to step up the ladder within the 3 years at GO-Events from being a casual waitress and bartender to a full-time eventing staff and Warehouse Manager. During the period that I have been casually working for GO-Events, I was a carpenter assistant, where I got to build garden benches, plant boxes, stools, and more. For my free days/weekends, I worked at the SPCA in Grahamstown where I cleaned kennels and worked in the office. In the season, I worked at a beach bar where I was a bartender and sometimes waitressing. I also did carcass searching for a 5-month contract at the wind turbines. My job was to look for bird and bat carcasses that are affected by the turbines. Data was collected by marking a waypoint on the GPS where the carcass was found to determine the actual cause of death and the distance from the turbine itself.

One thing I have learned about myself within the past experiences is that I absolutely love learning new things. I believe that Web Development would be able to offer what I am looking for. This is a forever learning field which I believe is perfect for me, especially since I have a designing eye. I want to create a webpage/app/animations that are visually pleasing, interactive, professional, and fun.

So far, I have been ticking my goals checklist within web development. My main goal is to be able to get familiar with back-end development and animations.
